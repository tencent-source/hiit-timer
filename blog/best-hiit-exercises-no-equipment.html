15 Best HIIT Exercises You Can Do Without Equipment

Workouts10 min readUpdated January 2025

No gym membership? No equipment? No problem. Some of the most effective HIIT workouts use nothing but your bodyweight. These 15 exercises will spike your heart rate, burn calories, and build functional strength—anywhere, anytime.

Full-Body Exercises

1
Burpees
High IntensityFull BodyCardio

The king of bodyweight HIIT exercises. Burpees work every major muscle group while spiking your heart rate to maximum. One of the most efficient calorie-burning movements.

Form Tips

  • Keep core tight throughout the movement
  • Land softly on the balls of your feet
  • Modify by stepping back instead of jumping if needed
2
Mountain Climbers
High IntensityCoreCardio

A plank on steroids. Mountain climbers blast your core while delivering serious cardiovascular benefits. Drive your knees toward your chest as fast as possible.

Form Tips

  • Keep hips level—don't let them bounce up and down
  • Hands directly under shoulders
  • Drive knees toward chest, not just forward
3
Jumping Jacks
Medium IntensityFull BodyCardio

Simple but effective. Perfect for warm-ups or as an active recovery exercise between more intense movements. Go fast for higher intensity.

Form Tips

  • Land softly with slight knee bend
  • Fully extend arms overhead
  • Keep core engaged throughout

Lower Body Exercises

4
Jump Squats
High IntensityLower BodyPower

Explosive lower body power. Drop into a squat, then explode upward as high as possible. Builds leg strength while torching calories.

Form Tips

  • Squat to at least parallel before jumping
  • Land softly, immediately descending into next squat
  • Swing arms for momentum
5
Lunge Jumps
High IntensityLower BodyPower

Alternating lunge jumps build unilateral leg power and coordination. One of the most demanding lower body exercises.

Form Tips

  • Keep front knee over ankle, not past toes
  • Land softly and absorb impact
  • Modify with reverse lunges if too intense
6
High Knees
High IntensityLower BodyCardio

Running in place with exaggerated knee lift. Drive knees to hip height as fast as possible. Great for sprint conditioning.

Form Tips

  • Drive knees up to hip level
  • Stay on balls of feet
  • Pump arms for speed
7
Skater Jumps
High IntensityLower BodyAgility

Lateral jumping from side to side, mimicking a speed skater. Builds lateral power and stability.

Form Tips

  • Land on one foot with slight knee bend
  • Reach opposite hand toward landing foot
  • Push off explosively to the other side

Upper Body Exercises

8
Push-Up to T-Rotation
Medium IntensityUpper BodyCore

Push-up followed by rotating into a side plank with arm extended. Works chest, shoulders, and obliques.

Form Tips

  • Keep hips level during rotation
  • Stack feet or stagger for balance
  • Extend arm straight up, look at hand
9
Diamond Push-Ups
Medium IntensityUpper BodyTriceps

Hands together in diamond shape under chest. Emphasizes triceps more than standard push-ups.

Form Tips

  • Form diamond with thumbs and index fingers
  • Keep elbows close to body
  • Lower chest to hands
10
Plyo Push-Ups
High IntensityUpper BodyPower

Explosive push-ups where hands leave the ground. Advanced exercise for upper body power.

Form Tips

  • Lower with control, explode up
  • Land with soft elbows
  • Modify by doing from knees if needed

Core Exercises

11
Bicycle Crunches
Medium IntensityCoreObliques

Alternating elbow-to-knee crunches with cycling motion. One of the most effective ab exercises according to research.

Form Tips

  • Touch elbow to opposite knee
  • Fully extend the other leg
  • Keep lower back pressed to floor
12
V-Ups
High IntensityCoreStrength

Lie flat, then simultaneously lift legs and torso to form a V shape. Advanced core exercise.

Form Tips

  • Keep legs straight if possible
  • Reach hands toward toes
  • Control the descent
13
Plank Jacks
High IntensityCoreCardio

In plank position, jump feet wide then back together. Combines core stability with cardio.

Form Tips

  • Keep hips stable—don't let them bounce
  • Engage core throughout
  • Land softly on balls of feet

Combination Exercises

14
Squat Thrusts
High IntensityFull BodyCardio

Burpee without the push-up. Squat, hands to floor, jump feet back to plank, jump feet forward, stand. Fast and effective.

Form Tips

  • Move as quickly as possible
  • Keep core engaged in plank
  • Land softly on each transition
15
Tuck Jumps
High IntensityFull BodyPower

Jump as high as possible while tucking knees to chest. Maximum intensity plyometric exercise.

Form Tips

  • Drive knees up, don't just lift feet
  • Land softly with bent knees
  • Reset fully between reps

Sample No-Equipment HIIT Workout

Try this 20-minute workout using exercises from this list:

  • Warm-up: 3 minutes of jumping jacks and high knees
  • Round 1: 30 seconds burpees, 15 seconds rest
  • Round 2: 30 seconds mountain climbers, 15 seconds rest
  • Round 3: 30 seconds jump squats, 15 seconds rest
  • Round 4: 30 seconds plyo push-ups, 15 seconds rest
  • Repeat 4x
  • Cool-down: 3 minutes stretching
