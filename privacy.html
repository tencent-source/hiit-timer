Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how HIIT Timer ("we", "our", or "the Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website.

Cookie Consent

We respect your privacy and give you control over your data. When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept All: Allow essential and marketing cookies (enables personalized ads)
  • Reject Marketing: Only allow essential cookies (no personalized ads)

Your consent choice is stored in your browser's local storage (not a cookie). You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser data or clicking the "Manage Cookie Preferences" link in our footer.

Information We Collect

HIIT Timer is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • No Personal Data: We do not require registration, login, or any personal information to use the timer.
  • Essential Cookies Only (by default): We only use cookies for essential functionality unless you consent to marketing cookies.
  • Local Storage: Your timer preferences and cookie consent choice are saved locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.

Google AdSense (Marketing Cookies)

Only loads if you consent. We use Google AdSense to monetize our free service. If you accept marketing cookies:

  • Google AdSense may set tracking cookies for personalized advertising
  • Google collects: IP address, device information, browsing behavior, ad interactions
  • Data is used to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance
  • Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

If you reject marketing cookies, AdSense will not load and no tracking occurs. You can also opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-Party Services

Our website includes these third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect usage data according to their privacy policy.
  • Amazon Associates: We participate in the Amazon Associates program. When you click affiliate links, Amazon may track your visit for commission purposes.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's minimal risk to your privacy. All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following data protection rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw cookie consent at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

Since we collect minimal data (only local browser storage and optional AdSense cookies), these rights are easily exercised by:

  • Clearing your browser's local storage and cookies
  • Clicking "Reject Marketing" in the cookie consent banner
  • Contacting us at the email below for assistance

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
